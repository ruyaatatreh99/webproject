BETHLEHEM INTERNATIONAL PERFORMING ARTS FESTIVAL

Bethlehem

27th October 2019

8 Am

The olive harvest season is one of the most interesting seasons in Palestine. Most of the families, students, tourist groups experience the art of olive picking. Celebrating this, The 18th Annual Olive Harvest Festival is held in Manger Square in Bethlehem. This year's theme is Rooted as an Olive Tree. This festival aims to highlight the symbolic, national and economic importance of the olive tree, which the Palestinians consider it a holy tree. The festival includes a variety of activities including an official ceremony and a market for selling olive products and other cultural and artistic activities.
